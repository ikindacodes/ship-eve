# Getting started with TaskFlow

TaskFlow helps teams plan work with projects, tasks, and shared views.

## Invite a teammate

To invite someone to your workspace:

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Go to **Team**.
3. Click **Invite member**.
4. Enter their email address and choose a role (**Member** or **Admin**).
5. Click **Send invite**.

Invited users receive an email with a link that expires after 7 days.

## Plans at a glance

| Plan | Seats | Projects |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | up to 3 | up to 5 |
| Pro | up to 25 | unlimited |
| Enterprise | custom | unlimited |

Free workspaces can upgrade to Pro from **Settings → Billing → Upgrade**.

## Create a project

1. Click **New project** on the home screen.
2. Name the project and pick a default view (**Board** or **List**).
3. Click **Create**.

## Delete a workspace

Only workspace **Owners** can delete a workspace.

1. Open **Settings → Workspace**.
2. Scroll to **Danger zone**.
3. Click **Delete workspace** and confirm the workspace name.

Deletion is permanent after 30 days.
