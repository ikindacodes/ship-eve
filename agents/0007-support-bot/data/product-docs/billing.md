# Billing and plans

TaskFlow billing is per workspace. Charges apply to paid seats on Pro and Enterprise plans.

## Pro plan pricing

Pro costs **$12 per user per month** when billed monthly.

Annual billing is **$120 per user per year** (two months free).

Upgrade from **Settings → Billing → Upgrade to Pro**.

## Free plan limits

The Free plan includes:

- up to **3 seats**
- up to **5 active projects**
- 1 GB file storage per workspace

When you hit a limit, TaskFlow prompts you to upgrade or remove unused projects.

## Refunds

TaskFlow can refund the **most recent Pro charge** if you contact **support@taskflow.app** within **14 days** of the charge.

TaskFlow does not refund third-party services (for example AWS, Google Cloud, or other vendors you use alongside TaskFlow).

## Invoices

Download invoices from **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. PDF copies are available for the last 24 months.
